BIO AND CONTACT: Dante Healy

I'm a Senior Finance Leader and Financial Transformation Project Specialist, developing and implementing end-to-end financial and business change strategies for dynamic agile businesses to drive growth.

As a certified change project leader, I effectively lead global change initiatives and deliver complex business analysis/process re-engineering while leveraging my comprehensive knowledge of integrated technology solutions to ensure compliance and drive process improvements through automation and integration. This recently includes leading a finance transformation programme at FCE UK to introduce a common, automated finance system that delivered $5.1m of cost savings p/a for Accounting across Europe.

I have a proven track record of rapidly identifying the critical drivers for cross-functional business success while establishing the necessary KPI's and reporting to optimise performance.
